# For Student Researchers

We are recruiting 2 student researchers to join this project (1 at Yale, 1 at Morehouse).

## What You'll Work On

This depends on your skills and interests, but expected contributions include:

- Downloading and organizing satellite imagery archives
- Labeling training data from known Sargassum bloom events
- Preprocessing and data pipeline development
- Dashboard development
- Fieldwork assistance during DR field campaigns (Summer 2026, Summer 2027)

## What We Expect

- Prior experience with Python
- Familiarity with remote sensing, geospatial data, or machine learning is a plus but not required
- We will onboard you
